Write universal instructions
Memocipe lets you write, document, collect and display all kinds of instructions.
This can not only be done for your cooking or baking receipts.
The universal format also allows instructions for DIY crafts, home repairs, work or any How-To you can imagine.

Make use of your smartphone
When a plain description text just isn't enaugh to document your instruction as accurate as needed, Memocipe will utilize a smartphones functionality to include more interactivity.
Until now you can make use of timer, reminder and notification to delegate some work back to the App.
More features will be included in future updates.
