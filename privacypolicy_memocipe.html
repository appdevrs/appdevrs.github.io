Privacy Policy of the Memoceipt iOS App


1st December 2020

Appdevrs respects the privacy of its users.
This Privacy Policy explains how and why the Memoceipt App does not store or collect any of your personal data.
If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Memoceipt App.

Memoceipt has no function that can be used to upload or send data to any destination on the internet.
There is no server used with Memoceipt that could be used to store any data.
After the installation of Memoceipt, the App will work offline.
There is no user database to store any data.
Memoceipt does not use additional third-party software.

Memoceipt will store data on the device it runs on in order to work as expected.
This data is no personal data and consists of files or configurations the user wishes to save on the device.

If a user asks to see stored personal data, Appdevrs will not be able to show anything, because there is no stored data.

You will be deemed to have been made aware of, and will be deemed to have accepted
this Privacy Policy by your continued use of the Memoceipt App.
