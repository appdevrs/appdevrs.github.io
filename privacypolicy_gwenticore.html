Privacy Policy


14th November 2018

Appdevrs respects the privacy of its users.
This Privacy Policy explains how the Gwenticore App doesn't store or collect any of your personal data.
If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Gwenticore App.
Gwenticore has no function that can be used to upload or send data to any destination on the internet.
There is no server that Gwenticore could use to store any data.
There is no user-database to store any data.
Gwenticore does not use additional third-party software.

Gwenticore will store data on the device it runs on, in order to work as expected.
This data is no personal data and consists of files the user wishes to save on the device.

If a user asks to see the stored data, Appdevrs will not be able to show anything, because there is no stored data.

You will be deemed to have been made aware of, and will be deemed to have accepted
this Privacy Policy by your continued use of the Gwenticore App.
